5th Floor:
  • The first floor holds one of Colgate's Dining Services: Chobani.
  • A back room that remains quiet throughout the room.
  • Four different computer labs.
  • A meeting room--Perfect for group projects!
  • A black color only printer
    • 4th Floor:
  • The fourth floor is perfect for studying and relaxing.
  • Holds single cubicles for when you need to concetrate and focus.
  • Group tables

    • 3rd Floor:
  • The third floor is perfect for when you are with friends studying!
  • Contains multiple group tables.
  • A color and black printer.
  • Two exits that lead to Little Hall as well as James B. Colgate Hall.

  • The second floor is similar to the 4th floor; perfect for studying and relaxing.
  • Holds single cubicles for when you need to concetrate and focus.
  • Group tables
  • Sofas for a more relaxed and comfortable setting next to the floor windows and dim lights.
    • 1st Floor:
  • The first floor could be considered one of the quietest places to study.
  • The only way to get in is by using the elevator, instead of an open entrance like the other floors.
  • Holds group tables as well as individual cubicles when not in use by Seniors.
  • It's just you, your thoughts, and your work!
